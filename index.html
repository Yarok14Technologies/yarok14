About Yarok14 Technologies

Yarok14 Technologies is a semiconductor and AI/ML engineering company specializing in advanced-node ASIC design and verification, UVM-based verification, DFT and DFx methodologies, physical design and implementation, RF engineering, FPGA prototyping, embedded systems, 5G/6G hardware platforms, and industrial AI solutions.

Our team has experience supporting sub-16nm FinFET and advanced low-power process technologies, enabling high-performance, energy-efficient, and area-optimized silicon solutions for AI acceleration and high-performance computing workloads.

We collaborate with global engineering teams to deliver secure, high-performance silicon and intelligent hardware systems across AI acceleration, robotics and drones, cloud computing infrastructure, cybersecurity and defense, agriculture technology, and renewable energy systems.

From architecture definition to tape-out and silicon validation, Yarok14 Technologies supports clients throughout the complete product lifecycle — helping startups and enterprises accelerate innovation in the rapidly evolving semiconductor and AI hardware ecosystem.
